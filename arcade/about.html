We are HiTech

Taking one new challenge after another, Hi-Tech today boasts of a pan-spectrum portfolio of Industrial Structures, Residential Buildings, Commercial Buildings and Institutional & Other buildings of various scales. Its credibility among clients and consultants is based on facts–
- It has a proven track record of delivering quality on time, for every project.
- It is one of the few construction companies in Ahmedabad with quality system certification ISO 9001:2008 and Govt. of Gujarat approved ‘AA’ class special category – I (Buildings)
- With an army of labourers and 275+ experienced and well trained professionals, Hi-Tech is capable of responding to complex challenges.

Our VISION

To be the first preferred company for customized construction services in the industry.

Our MISSION

To provide quality construction and timely completion through enhanced construction tactics and practice with total commitment.



Our Strengths

Qualified Leadership

Hi-Tech’s promoters are highly qualified Civil Engineers who know not only the business of construction but also the craft of building. This helps them understand the finer nuances of construction and turn clients’ visions into realities.

Adopting the best from the ‘Big’

Hi-Tech has been quick to adopt best practices in the industry in systems and processes. It has excellent project planning and monitoring systems and well established quality and safety protocols.

Qualified and Experienced Execution Team

Qualified leaders make qualified teams. Hi-Tech has invested in its people and created trained professionals from the smallest to highest levels of the organisation so that good decisions are made at each level.

Retaining the best from ‘Small’

Even as it grows, Hi-Tech retains some of the best advantages of being small – being cost-effective and flexible. With a project-specific working approach, it is able to adapt to the needs of the clients and respond to emergent needs of the project.

Constant Technological Upgradation

Hi-Tech has continually invested in upgrading its Construction Technology.
